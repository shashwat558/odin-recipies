Samosa Recipe

Description

The samosas are a fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, lentils.

Sweet samosas are also sold in the cities of Pakistan including Peshawar; these sweet samosas contain no filling and are dipped in thick sugar syrup.

ingredients

  • Refined Wheat flour(Maida)
  • potatoes
  • Peas
  • Green chilli
  1. Step 1- Mix the flour, Water and salt together(not be very diluted)
  2. Step 2- Mash the boiled potatoes
  3. Step 3- Mix the mashed potatoes with salt and other spices
  4. Step 4- Add some red chilli and cutted green chilli pieces to the step3 mixture.
  5. Step 5- Mix it well
  6. Step 6- Now with that flour mixture make hollow pyramid like structure
  7. Step 7- Now fill the Potato filling inside that.
  8. Step 8- Seal its open ends and parts with flour mixture only.
  9. Step 9- Now Deep fry Your Samosa in oil.
