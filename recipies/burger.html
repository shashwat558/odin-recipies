Burger recipe

Description

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun.

Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

ingredients

  • Burger bun
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Potato patty
  • Cabbage
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps-

  1. Step 1- Firstly, take the burger buns and put some ketchup on them or may be some cheese if you like.
  2. Step 2- Now, add some seasonigs on the bun.
  3. Step 3- On the bun place tomato, cabbage leafs and any other vegetable you may like.
  4. Step 4- Now place the patty
  5. Step 5- On the top of patty, apply some cheese
  6. Step 6- Repeat second Step on the top of cheese
  7. Step 7- Lastly place the other bun on the top.
  8. Step 8- Put it on a pan in some medium or high heat.
